Over the Years

2011 - Now

The Recovery

There's been growth in Online Banking projects. Highlights include leading a team of BA's in delivering new registration and sign-on capabilities, ensuring the stability of the system as well as looking at user and platform coexistence.

Another standout was the Securities Exchange STP project where I partnered with the business and technology to drive requirements / design / solution end-to-end. I even managed to work with business development to help produce new market products.

Finally I was involved in a Customer Profitability project where collectively we were able to deliver the solution that sources, calculates and stores bank-wide data. Personally I was able to obtain sign-off on the methodology document at GM-level on first iteration.







2008 - 2010

The Crises

A challenging period but I managed to pick up work in Risk Management integrating the systems, processes and credit scoring capabilities between 2 major banks.

I was also engaged as a consultant in 2 pieces of work: a Liquidity Management (Treasury) and Conveyance / Property Settlement program. We specified the functional specs, use cases, process and data requirements for the clients.

A Reinsurance firm came knocking and I was fortunate to work closely with actuarial, business development and IT teams to develop a product database prototype and specs for cash flow and reserve calcs.







2001 - 2007

The Wonder Years

I was in the midst of an Equity Derivatives project just as GFC hit. I worked my way within Front-Middle-Back Office areas to produce process maps. I was also involved in a lot of tech initiatives for an Investment Bank.

Still a standout in my career has been the Basel 2 program in Institutional Banking. The work was cast far and wide - Securitisation, Repos, Off-Balance Sheet items etc. The work was complex but the team was superb.

My project career started in Retail Banking and Superannuation managing changes to the Loans system and migrating Super & Insurance products from Melbourne to Sydney.
